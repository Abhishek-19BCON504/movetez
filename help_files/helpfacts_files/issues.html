My driver was rude

If you have any issues regarding the behaviour of driver, then you can complain us from our Contact Us page.

Or we contact you after raising a ticket by filling the forum in support section.



Issues related with goods

If you/helpers have forgotten to unload any of the goods at the destination and as a result the goods are lost in the vehicle, in that case you can raise a ticket in support section and mentioning the vehicle number there in the 'issue' textarea given in the forum.


My driver refused my destination

If the booking is confirmed, then refusal of destination from driver side is unwanted and against our norms. Therefore, you can surely raise a ticket in support section or you can contact us by getting our contact information from Contact us page
