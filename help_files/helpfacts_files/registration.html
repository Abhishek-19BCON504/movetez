Register as a delivery partner

  • Open the MoveTez partner app if you want to register as a delivery partner. You can get the android app from the google playstore.

  • On the homescreen of app, accept all the required hardware accesses or permissions (if any).

  • After selecting your language, enter your mobile number and verify it using OTP.

  • After confirming your mobile number, you will be registered as a delivery partner. Now you have to complete your basic details for making finalizations.

Fill your basic details:

  • You will be required to enter your full name in two input boxes as first name and last name.

  • Enter your email id in the 'email-id' input box (which is optional).

  • Then enter your permanent address.

  • Then click on next.

Submit your documents:

  • You have to upload your driving license. Finally, you will have to upload your vehicle registration certificate.

Fill your vehicle details:

  • After uploading your documents, fill your vehicle details by selecting your vehicle type from the dropdown menu and then enter your plate number and then select vehicle zone type.

    The vehicle zone type contains another dropdown menu having three options - All India Permit, Rajasthan Permit, Not Applicable. Select your desired type.

  • You will also have to upload your vehicle pictures.

Fill the material categories:

  • After filling the vehicle details, go to the material category where you will have to input all the details regarding the material you can transport taking your vehicle into consideration.

  • Also, you will be asked to enter your work experience duration in the field you opt in terms of years.

Final step:

After clicking on confirm, you will be under document verification process.

After successful verification of your documents, you'll be registered as a delivery partner.
