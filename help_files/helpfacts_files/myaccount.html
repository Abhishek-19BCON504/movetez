Update your mobile number

  • Open the MoveTez app from the app menu and tap on the three-dot options icon on the top-right corner of your app window.

  • Touch on the mobile number to change it or navigate to settings and there you will find an option to change your mobile number. There you can update your mobile number

  • Enter your new mobile number and verify it via OTP sent to that number by SMS.

  • You have successfully updated your mobile number.


Troubleshooting

Not receiving SMS

If you are encountering any problems like not receiving SMS on your mobile number, then here are the possible reasons for that:

  • That mobile number may not be your registered mobile number.

  • The mobile network carriers of that number may have barred the incoming services for the number.

  • Your mobile inbox maybe full.


Try to solve these problems and if the problem still persists, you can try to get OTP through call by tapping on 'get otp via call'.

App crashing

  • If the app crashes, you can clear the app data or temporary cache of the app.



  • If the issue still persists, re-install the app, check for any updates.

Unable to book from website

If you encountered problems during booking, you can clear your browser history or clear cookies. Also you can have issues due to connectivity related problems such as browsing speed, browser, etc. That is why, it is recommended to use google chrome, firefox 49.0 (or newer), microsoft edge, IE 10.0 (or newer) or Apple's Safari (15.0 or newer).

Account Blocked

Your account may be blocked due to any unusual activity related to your device. You can complaint us by navigating to Support section. There you will find all the neccesary contact information.
