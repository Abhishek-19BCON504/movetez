Book a ride from our app

  • Open the movetez app from your apps menu. You can get the android app from the google playstore.

  • On the homescreen of app, accept all the required accesses and permissions and then touch the button “Get Started”.

  • After selecting your language, enter your mobile number. After verifying it via OTP, you will be registered as a user.

  • Complete your profile or you can do it anytime later by touching the three-dot options icon found on the top-right corner of your app window.

  • Now you will see the list of all available vehicles. Select any of the vehicle as per your needs.

  • On the same screen, there you will see two options – Regular and Reserve. Choose as per your requirement.

If you want to book as regular:

If you want to book a vehicle for fixed duration of time irrespect of the distance travelled, then choose reserve otherwise booking as regular is the best choice.

  • Assuming you have checked for regular booking, after selecting your vehicle, you'll be taken to a set of three options - Seperate, Pool, Luxury

For Seperate:

  • For seperate, you are allowed only single pickup as well as drop location.

  • Select the pickup and drop locations from the map provided.

  • Tap on continue.

  • After that, select your material type you wish to port and the number of helpers required from the 'helper requirement' list option.

  • Tap the 'Book Now' button and then touch on 'confirm' button for finalization.

  • Your booking will be confirmed and the driver details will be displayed on your screen.

  • You can call or message the driver or even cancel the booking (before ETA).

  • You will also receive a code which you'll have to share it to your driver for starting the trip.

  • Once the code is shared, the trip will get started and you can track your order from the on-screen map.

  • You can even do the payment from the payments option. Various payment methods are discussed in payments section.

For Pool:

  • For pool, you are allowed multiple pickup locations and single drop location or vice-versa.

  • You will be provided two options- Multiple Pickup (single drop) or Multiple Drop (single pickup). Choose any one as per your choice.

  • Fill the pickup and drop location(s)

  • After that, select your material type you wish to port and the number of helpers required from the 'helper requirement' list option.

  • Tap the 'Book Now' button and then touch on 'confirm' button for finalization.

  • Your booking will be confirmed and the driver details will be displayed on your screen.

  • You can call or message the driver or even cancel the booking (before ETA).

  • You will also receive a code which you'll have to share it to your driver for starting the trip.

  • Once the code is shared, the trip will get started and you can track your order from the on-screen map.

  • You can even do the payment from the payments option. Various payment methods are discussed in payments section.

If you want to book as reserve:

If you want to book a vehicle for fixed duration of time irrespect of the distance travelled, then booking as reserve is the best choice otherwise switch to book as regular.

  • Assuming you have checked for reserved booking, after selecting your vehicle, you'll be taken to a set of three options - Seperate, Pool, Luxury

  • You will be asked reservation timing, material types and helper requirement. In the reservation timing field, you have to choose the amount of time you want to reserve the vehicle. In the helper requirement field, you have to select the number of helpers required for your job to get done.

  • Tap on Next button.

  • Confirm by clicking on Book button for finalization.

  • Your booking will be confirmed and the driver details will be displayed on your screen.

  • You can call or message the driver or even cancel the booking (before ETA).

  • You will also receive a code which you'll have to share it to your driver for starting the trip.

  • Once the code is shared, the trip will get started and you can track your order from the on-screen map.

  • You can even do the payment from the payments option. Various payment methods are discussed in payments section.

  • If you reached with your time limit, a popup will be displayed on your screen which asks you to extend time. Select your next time limit upto you want to extend and click on 'Submit and Update' button afterwards.


Book a ride from our website

  • Navigate to https://www.movetez.com on your browser.

  • On the homepage, you will see a list of all available vehicles. You can click on any vehicle to see its details.

  • After suiting yourself for a vehicle, click on 'Book Now' button.

  • You will be redirected to bookings page where you will be asked to enter your mobile number for booking.

  • After confirming your mobile number, click on continue and after verifying via OTP, you'll be registered on movetez.com as a user.

  • After selecting vehicle as per your requirement, select an option from 'Book as regular' or 'Book as reserve'.
