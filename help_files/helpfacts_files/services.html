Regular Service:

In the regular service, we provide vehicles for goods transportation for our customers providing with the best possibilities from our side like seperate, pool and luxury systems so that one can feel free and safe no matter how valuable the goods are. The goods are protected in the vehicles from our strict security system. Moreover the vehicle can be tracked any time from the tracking Id assigned to the order.

The pool services allow you to choose multiple drop and pickup locations for your transportation of goods no matter the multiple number of places your goods belong to by considering the space availability in the vehicle. Also, you can transport multiple material types you wish without even bothering about it.

The luxury system is designed for your valuable goods such as electronic items like smartphones, laptop, etc dependent on the size of the box provided for the goods. The luxury service is provided by a protection based on fully digital security system that works on OTP - verification as a result of which you need not to worry for your prestigious the goods are.

Reserve service:

In the reserve service, we provide vehicles for our customers for a fixed duration of time which is obviously set by themselves. If the time selected by the customer exceeds the limit, we provide facility to extend it to a certain limit of time.
